Tips & shortcuts

  • Connect — click a machine card.
  • Move to a tag — drag a card onto a tag section.
  • Add to multiple tags — hold ⌘ / Ctrl / Alt while dragging.
  • Edit / delete — hover a card or tag for the ✎ / 🗑 icons.
  • Recolor a tag — click its color dot.
  • Fold a group — click the tag header (or its ▸ chevron).
  • Nest tags — name a tag Parent/Child.
